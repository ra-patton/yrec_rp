We include 4 different lower MS cases for stars between 0.09 and 1 Msun, and one set of models for brown dwarfs between 0.03 and 0.08 Msun. All are solar metallicity, with the GS98 mixture. The cases, and the mass ranges covered, are as follows; #p# in the case summary indicates that multiple masses are given.

SamplelowerMS_m#p#feh+0p0GS98_allard_15Gyr - Allard SBC / OPAL EoS - brown dwarfs 0.03-0.08 Msun, evolved to 15 Gyr or when they drop below the Allard table range. Cases are 0p03, 0p04, 0p05, 0p06, 0p07, 0p08.
SamplelowerMS_m#p#feh+0p0GS98_allard_ZAMS - Allard SBC / OPAL EoS - stars 0.09-1 Msun. Cases are 0p09, 0p1, 0p15, 0p2, 0p3, 0p4, 0p5, 0p6, 0p7, 0p8, 0p9, 1p0.
SamplelowerMS_m#p#feh+0p0GS98_base_ZAMS - grey atmosphere SBC / OPAL EoS - stars 0.1 - 1 Msun. Cases are 0p1, 0p15, 0p2, 0p3, 0p4, 0p5, 0p6, 0p7, 0p8, 0p9, 1p0.
SamplelowerMS_m#p#feh+0p0GS98_kurucz_ZAMS - Kurucz SBC / OPAL EoS - stars 0.15 - 1 Msun. Cases are 0p15, 0p2, 0p3, 0p4, 0p5, 0p6, 0p7, 0p8, 0p9, 1p0. Lower mass cases are outside of the Kurucz table range.
SamplelowerMS_m#p#feh+0p0GS98_yaleeos_ZAMS - grey atmosphere / Yale Eos - stars 0.3 - 1 Msun. Cases are 0p3, 0p4, 0p5, 0p6, 0p7, 0p8, 0p9, 1p0.Lower mass cases have convergence difficulties.

The Run files are sample Linux batch scripts used to generate model grids.
