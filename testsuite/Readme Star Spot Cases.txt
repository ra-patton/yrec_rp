We provide three sample cases that include star spots (m0p1 feh+0.0 spot25 ZAMS; m0p3 feh+0.0 spot25 ZAMS; m1p0 feh+0.0 spot25 ZAMS) in the test suite. The models are solar abundance,  0.1, 0.3 and 1.0 Msun, and they are evolved from the deuterium burning birthline to the ZAMS. These use the same physics as the base grid, with the exception of a star spot filling factor of 0.25 and a temperature ratio between spotted and unspotted regions of 0.85.
