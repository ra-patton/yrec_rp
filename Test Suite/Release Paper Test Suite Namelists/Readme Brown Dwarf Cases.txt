We provide three sample namelists for brown dwarfs in the test suite (m0p03 feh+0.0 allard 15 Gyr; m0p05 feh+0.0 allard 15 Gyr; m0p08 feh+0.0 allard 15 Gyr). The models are solar abundance, and they are evolved from the deuterium burning birthline to 15 Gyr or whenever they run outside of the bounds of the atmospheres table. These use the same physics as the base grid with the exception of using the Allard atmospheres as a surface boundary condition (all 3) and using only the SCV EoS, instead of OPAL (only the 0.08 Msun case).
