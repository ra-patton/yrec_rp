These namelists are examples where selective nuclear reaction rates are modified. We include 3 cases for a 1 solar mass model and 3 cases for a 9 solar mass model; both are evolved from the DBl to the ZAMS, and both are solar metallicity.

The cases presented are

Sample_Class2_m1p0feh+0p0_stdpp_ZAMS - Solar mass, standard nuclear reaction rates;
Sample_Class2_m1p0feh+0p0_s11m2_ZAMS - Solar mass, pp rate dropped by a factor of 100;
Sample_Class2_m1p0feh+0p0_ppm2_ZAMS - Solar mass, pp, He3+He3, and He3+He4 rates all dropped by a factor of 100.

Sample_Class2_m9p0feh+0p0_stdcno_ZAMS - 9 solar masses, standard nuclear reaction rates;
Sample_Class2_m9p0feh+0p0_cnom3_ZAMS - 9 solar masses, N14+p rate dropped by a factor of 1000;
Sample_Class2_m9p0feh+0p0_cnom3_ZAMS - 9 solar masses, C12+p, C13+p, N14+p, N15+p (both branches), O16+p cross sections all dropped by a factor of 1000.
